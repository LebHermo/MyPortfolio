Basilisk Shirt Design

University of Immaculate Conception
Sep 19, 2024
Character Design

For this project, I created shirt designs for a university’s intramurals event, featuring illustrations of four iconic mythical creatures: the basilisk, manticore, sphynx, and pegasus. The theme of the event centered around these legendary mythical creatures, and I brought them to life with engaging visuals.

Working with the colors provided by the client, I created a design that balanced minimalism and modernism, making it both university-friendly and visually striking. The final product was a vibrant, contemporary shirt that students proudly wore during the event, perfectly capturing the spirit of the competition.
